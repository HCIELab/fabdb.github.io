FabGalaxy: A Novel Visualization for Exploring Fabrication Research

Team Members: Liang He (lianghe@cs.washington.edu), Kate Jung (sjung94@uw.edu)



FabGalaxy Online Demo: https://cse512-18s.github.io/fabviz/

Abstract

Fabrication, as a young research field, has been increasingly growing in HCI andGraphics in the past decade. There is no existing search engines or tools for inexperienced junior researchers to know the overall research status, locate the related work, recognize the big brains in this field, and explore potential research topics in fabrication. Inspired by the online repository created by the HCI Engineering Group from MIT CSAIL, we explore a visual and interactive way to present the research work in fabrication. In our tool, we use author and keyword centered search approach and provide a multi-dimensional dot cluster design to visualize all work in fabrication research.
